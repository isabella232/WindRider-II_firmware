WindRider Firmware Guide
========================
This document is a comprehensive guide to installation and development of Wind Rider Firmware. The project is based on STM32F103C8T6 microcontroller and STM's official peripheral library. Download this tutorial as `pdf <https://github.com/ccny-ros-pkg/WindRider-II_firmware/tree/gh-pages/windrider_firmware.pdf>`_.
